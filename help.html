Open the login page, select your role, and enter your registered email and password to access the portal.

How do I check attendance?

Use the dashboard view to review attendance summaries, history records, and recent notices.

Use the support contact information provided below or consult the department office for assistance.

Use the forgot password option on the login page to begin the reset flow.
